The 3 signals.
Memorise these.
Your early warning system — for the rest of your life. No app required.
😴
Sleep degrades
→ Fix sleep first. No screens –1h for 5 days. Everything else follows.
⚡
Can't switch off
→ Double the breathing sessions. Morning + midday + evening. For 5 days.
🌀
Overthinking returns
→ Reinstate the worry window. Same time, every day. Let it do its job.
