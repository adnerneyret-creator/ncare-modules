What happens when hunger returns
Ghrelin rises. GLP-1 suppressed your hunger hormone. Without it, ghrelin bounces back — often above your original baseline. This is temporary. It stabilises in 4–8 weeks.
Fullness signals weaken. Leptin — the satiety hormone — takes longer to register. You may feel hungry again 1–2 hours after eating. This is hormonal, not a character flaw.
The reward system reactivates. Food becomes more rewarding again. Cravings feel stronger. This is the dopamine system returning to baseline — not a relapse.
The window of vulnerability is weeks 2–6 after stopping GLP-1. If you build habits during this window, they become your new baseline. That's exactly what Maintain is designed for.

When did you last eat a proper meal?

✓ Physical hunger
Your body needs fuel. Eat a balanced meal — protein + fibre. Don't wait. Ignoring physical hunger leads to overeating at the next meal.
→ Emotional hunger
This isn't your stomach talking — it's your brain seeking comfort. Use the STOP method from Module 04. Or go to the 10-minute protocol on the next slide.
The 10-minute protocol.
Before you decide to eat — do this first.
1
Drink a full glass of water
Wait 2 minutes. Dehydration is mistaken for hunger in about 30% of cases. This one step resolves a third of all "false hunger" signals.
2
Ask: physical or emotional?
Use the 2-question test from the previous slide. If emotional — go to step 3. If physical — go eat a real meal.
3
Wait 10 minutes — actively
Go outside. Do 20 squats. Call someone. Change your environment. The hunger will either pass or clarify. Either outcome is useful information.
4
Decide consciously
You've now made the choice deliberate. Whether you eat or not — it's a decision, not a reaction. That's the only difference that matters long-term.
5 foods that actually work.
Keep these at home. They handle 80% of hunger situations.
🥚
Eggs
Highest satiety per calorie. 2 eggs = 2–3h of fullness.
🥜
Almonds
Fat + protein combo. Slows glucose absorption. 15 almonds = enough.
🫘
Lentils
Highest fibre-to-hunger ratio of any food. Prep once, eat all week.
🥑
Avocado
Monounsaturated fat activates CCK — the satiety hormone. Half an avocado is a meal anchor.
🍵
Green tea
EGCG mildly suppresses ghrelin. L-theanine reduces stress-driven eating. The best non-food tool in the pantry.
