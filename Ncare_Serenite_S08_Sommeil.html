Deep sleep (N3) repairs the body and resets cortisol. REM processes emotions and consolidates memory. Chronic stress suppresses both — you sleep but don't recover.
The tired-but-wired pattern is classic N3 suppression. High evening cortisol blocks deep sleep entry. You sleep 7 hours but feel like 4.
The 7-day sleep
rebuild protocol.
One change per evening. Cumulative effect within a week.
–3h
Last intense exercise
Evening HIIT raises core temp and cortisol for 3 hours. Gentle movement only after this point.
–2h
Last heavy meal
Digestion raises core temperature. Sleep onset requires body temp to drop. Give it 2 hours.
–1h
Screens off — dim lights
Blue light and cognitive stimulation block melatonin. One hour is the minimum deactivation window.
